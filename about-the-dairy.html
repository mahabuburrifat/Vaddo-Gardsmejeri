FROM GARDEN TO STOMACH!

WELCOME
TO THE DAIRY!

In the farm's own dairy, we protect the natural short food chain from pasture to stomach. Here we use Swedish milk raw materials to produce Väddö Grill Cheeseburgers, Farm Cheese, Farm Ice Cream, Väddö Cheddar and more.

HAPPY COWS GIVE
THE HIGHEST
QUALITY

Our philosophy is as simple as it gets, healthy and happy cows come first. It provides the highest quality milk and thus the best products.

TRADITIONAL FARM
& MODERN FOOD
CRAFTSMANSHIP

At the farm, we are still driven by the same values ​​as when we started in 1912. We believe that quality comes first and that the natural always beats the artificial. Our ingredients are of the highest quality, which in turn produces fantastic products.

TRUST THROUGH
TRANSPARENCY
