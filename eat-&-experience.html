BONDEN & BURGAREN

At Bonden & Burgaren we serve smash burgers using the farm's own KRAV organic meat, the farm's unique organic burger cheddar and bread from a local baker. The dairy's KRAV-organic Grill Cheeseburger is on the menu as a vegetarian option and also as a side dish in the form of crispy deep-fried cubes. When our fantastic farm chefs build your burger, it will also be very tasty and a winning combo with the ingredients sustainability, quality, passion and culinary craftsmanship. Raw materials without unnecessary additives and transport. No frills just real.
It is not the table reservation, but just passing by. The restaurant is cashless, which means that we only accept payment by card.

Opening hours:

Bonden & Burgaren is located by the water at Väddö Kanal and the address is Grisslehamnsvägen 47, Väddö

Open:
Every day at 11-20
