CHEW BY CHEW TOWARDS A BETTER FOOD CULTURE

A REAL FARM!

Our KRAV organic farm is located on the island of Väddö in Roslagen. Here at Väddö Gårdsmejeri,
you get a unique experience from pasture to belly where you get to see up close how food is really made without shortcuts. Welcome!

OVER GENERATIONS
– THE THOUGHT PERSISTS

The historic farm has been owned and farmed since 1912 by the Edlund family - Hasse and Yvonne together with their three daughters and sons-in-law. In other words, you are treated to a family farm feeling at its best. The modern agriculture is organic and KRAV-labelled since then, and in 2005 the farm dairy was started. Today, the farm has 60 cows that supply milk for the production of cheese and ice cream.

HEALTH OF OUR MOST IMPORTANT
EMPLOYEES

In our rural idyll, you can meet cows, pigs and other animals and see how the farm's good products are produced in the dairy. You get the opportunity to see and feel for yourself how close it can be between green meadows, happy cows and really good food. It's just like in the food industry's advertising pictures. For real though.
