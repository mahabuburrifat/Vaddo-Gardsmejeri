REFUND POLICY

Welcome to Farm to Fest. We value our customers and aim to provide the best products and customer service possible. This refund policy outlines the conditions and procedures for refunds and exchanges.

    General Conditions

  • Eligibility: To be eligible for a refund, the item must be unused, in the same condition as received, and in its original packaging.
  • Proof of Purchase: A receipt or proof of purchase is required for all returns and refunds.

    Time Frame for Returns

  • Perishable Goods: Returns must be initiated within 48 hours of delivery due to the nature of perishable goods.
  • Non-Perishable Goods: Returns must be initiated within 14 days of receipt.

    Non-Refundable Items

  • Gift cards
  • Downloadable software products
  • Some health and personal care items
  • Any item not in its original condition, is damaged, or missing parts for reasons not due to our error.

    Refund Process

  • Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you of the status of your refund.
  • If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within a certain amount of days.

    Late or Missing Refunds

  • If you haven’t received a refund yet, first check your bank account again.
  • Then contact your credit card company; it may take some time before your refund is officially posted.
  • Next, contact your bank. There is often some processing time before a refund is posted.
  • If you’ve done all of this and still have not received your refund, please contact us at farmtofest@gmail.com.

    Exchanges

  • We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange it for the same item, send us an email at farmtofest@gmail.com. and send your item to Contact us.

    Return Shipping

  • You will be responsible for paying for your own shipping costs for returning your item. Shipping costs are non-refundable.
  • Depending on where you live, the time it may take for your exchanged product to reach you may vary.

    Contact Information

  • For any questions about our refund policy, contact us at farmtofest@gmail.com. or call us at 178807188.

    Changes to This Policy

  • We reserve the right to modify this refund policy at any time, so please review it frequently. Changes and clarifications will take effect immediately upon their posting on the website.
