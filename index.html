WELCOME
TO A REAL FARM

The farm has been run by the Edlund family since 1912. Here we work KRAV-ecologically and sustainably with the entire chain from pasture to stomach.
From the work in the field, to the farm's own milk production, further into the dairy to be processed into our farm products. The whole farm has a circular approach and today there are around 60 happy cows on the farm.

On the farm, you can visit our animals in Lilla Bondgården, shop in the farm shop, have a coffee in our Dairy Café, eat at Bonden & Burgaren or enjoy the farm's ice cream at Bondens Glasskalas, beautifully located by Väddö Kanal.
