DAIRY FARM SHOP

Welcome to us
In the farm shop you will find our farm-produced products and deli products from local neighbors.

We also have furnishings and toys.
In the dairy café, you can enjoy the farm's ice cream, coffee and hearty sandwiches.

The farmer's lunch is served weekdays between 10.30 and 14.00

Opening hours:


Farm shop, Dairy cafe & Lilla Bondgården
Every day at 10-17

DAIRY CAFE

Have a coffee or lunch in the farm's idyllic Mejericafé.

Here you will find treats in the form of coffee, hearty sandwiches, the dairy's own cheese pie & cheesecake, farm-produced ice cream, etc.

LITTLE FARMHOUSE

You will find Lilla Bondgården next to the Mejericaféet's garden. Lilla Bondgården is a perfect playground for small farmers.

Here you can ride a pedal tractor, swing and greet the farm's small animals during the summer season.
